About Me

I've always dreamed of being a hacker...

I first started trying to learn web development in 2017 using popular resources such as freeCodeCamp and the OdinProject. During that time and since, I've been working in the financial industry with dreams of breaking into the tech world but really not seeing a clear path to making a career switch. Well, a few months ago that path revealed itself to me by way of Per Scholas and their Software Engineering bootcamp. With the knowledge I've gained from this bootcamp, I not only feel like breaking into the tech world is possible for me but also that I will make a meaningful contribution to the tech world once I do break into it.

Over the past few months, I've had the pleasure of receiving hands-on technical training in building and deploying full stack web applications utilizing the MERN (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js) stack. This training greatly enhanced my understanding of web development and now I can use my love for creativity in conjunction with my love for logic to design & program dynamic & interactive webpages with user experience in mind. I am actively seeking to join a software development team where I can make an immediate impact while also continuing to learn and grow as a developer.
